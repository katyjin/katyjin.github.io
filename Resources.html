Provisional Rules of Procedure of the Security Council

Article 30 of the Charter says that the Security Council shall adopt its own rules of procedure, and in 1946, the Council adopted its Provisional Rules of Procedure.

Rules and Procedure of General Assembly

The Rules of Procedure of the General Assembly is available online. The official issuance is available as document A/520/Rev.20. Page 1 of A/C.1/59/PV.17 is an example of how the Chair explains the order of proceedings.

Publications

  1. MUN Organization Websites
    Check the websites of major MUN organizations and conferences. They often publish newsletters, reports, and articles related to their conferences and global issues.
  2. MUN Research Journals
    Some universities and MUN organizations publish research journals or magazines that feature articles, analyses, and opinions on various international issues.
  3. Online MUN Magazines and Blogs
    Explore online platforms and blogs that focus on MUN-related content. These may include articles on conference experiences, research insights, and commentary on international issues.
  4. MUN-related Academic Journals
    Some academic journals may feature articles related to Model United Nations, particularly in the fields of international relations, diplomacy, and education.
  5. Social Media and Forums
    Explore MUN-related groups on social media platforms or forums where participants share their experiences, research, and insights.
  6. University MUN Programs
    Check if universities with strong MUN programs have online repositories or publications featuring the research and contributions of their students in the field of Model UN.
