Model United Nations

Events


Events are simulations of the United Nations where students take on the roles of delegates representing different countries and debate and negotiate on various global issues. These events are typically organized by schools, universities, or non-profit organizations and provide a platform for students to develop their public speaking, negotiation, and diplomacy skills. MUN events aim to increase awareness of international relations and foster a sense of global citizenship among participants. The events usually include committee sessions, general assemblies, and social events, and are a great way for students to engage in discussions about real-world problems and practice diplomacy in a controlled environment.
