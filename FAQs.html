Model United Nations

How do I register to participate in Model UNs?

The United Nations does not organize Model UN conferences. Instead, search for local organizers online or through your school. You may also ask the United Nations Information Centre in your area for local opportunities and resources.

How can I contribute to the work of the UN as a student?

There are many ways for young people to engage with the United Nations. For example:

  1. United Nations Internship
  2. United Nations Volunteers
  3. Opportunities from the Office of the Secretary-General’s Envoy on Youth
  4. United Nations Academic Impact
  5. Take action for the Sustainable Development Goals
  6. Other youth opportunities at the UN

How does MUN work?

Participants, acting as delegates, research and represent a specific country in a UN committee. They debate, negotiate, and draft resolutions to address global challenges.

Why participate in MUN?

MUN provides a unique educational experience, fostering skills such as public speaking, negotiation, research, and diplomacy. It also increases awareness of global issues.

What is a resolution in MUN?

A resolution is a formal proposal drafted and debated by delegates to address a specific issue. It outlines solutions and actions that the committee should take.

How are awards determined in MUN?

Awards are often given based on delegates' performance, including their contributions to debate, negotiation skills, and the effectiveness of their resolutions.

Can you sponsor our Model UN event or provide a scholarship for participants?

The United Nations does not provide financial assistance of any kind for students participating in Model UN events and activities.
